About Us

Is your property in need of a fresh coat of paint? Do you want to add to the value of your home? If so, call Aquarelle Painting & Services Inc right away! We are a fully licensed and insured painting and pressure washing company operating throughout the entire Boston area. It is our first and foremost priority to earn your respect and repeat business. We want you to be a customer for life! Regardless of the complexity of the job you have for us, be it a painting or pressure washing service project, we have what it takes to ensure that your needs are met. Our extensively trained craftsmen are dedicated to making sure that you, our valuable clients, are 100% satisfied the service you are provided. We believe that challenging tasks are a chance to prove our professionalism and unsurpassed expertise. When working with us, you are sure to get impeccable results without the outrageous fees! Our Services

What we are best at

Exterior Painting

If the home is where the heart is, then you want your home to look great. It all starts with a fantastic exterior. From exterior touchups to full home recoating we cover it all.

Interior Painting

Time to get rid of the crayon marks and dings in the walls with a fresh coat. Whether you want to simple refresh a room or pick a totally new color we can help.

Kitchen and Cabinet Refinishing

You don't need to replace cabinets as soon as you might think. We can make your old cabinets look like new for a fraction of the cost

Drywall and Plastering

Fixing drywall looks easy on YouTube but it takes expertise to ensure the patch is not visible and blends in with the wall. We have years of expertise in fixing patches from small to large.

Pressure Washing

From patios to home exteriors pressure washing can bring the live back to your property. Remove dirt that builds up in the harsh Boston climate in a few hours.

General Carpentry

From small handyman jobs to larger installations we are experts beyond just a paint brush.
